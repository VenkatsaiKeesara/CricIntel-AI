# CricIntel AI - Dataset Design

## Project Overview

CricIntel AI is an AI-powered Business Intelligence platform developed to analyze IPL ticket sales and provide actionable business insights for franchise management.

The platform focuses on understanding ticket revenue, customer booking behavior, seat category performance, booking trends, and stadium occupancy using SQL, Python, Power BI, and Generative AI.

Since real IPL ticket booking data is not publicly available, realistic synthetic datasets will be created to simulate actual business operations.

# Datasets Used

The project consists of three datasets.

## Dataset 1 – Matches

Business Purpose

Stores information about every IPL match conducted during the season.

Primary Key

Match_ID

Expected Rows

100

---

## Dataset 2 – Customers

Business Purpose

Stores customer demographic information used for customer segmentation and booking behavior analysis.

Primary Key

Customer_ID

Expected Rows

50,000

---

## Dataset 3 – Ticket Sales

Business Purpose

Stores every ticket booking transaction generated by customers.

Primary Key

Ticket_ID

Foreign Keys

Match_ID

Customer_ID

Expected Rows

100,000
